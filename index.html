THE LAST GUARDIAN

About D.O.M.O.

THE DEPARTMENT
KEEPS EVERYTHING.

The Department of Miscellaneous Objects is an archive of the curious, the forgotten, and the beautifully ordinary. We believe every object carries the weight of the hands that held it and the stories that shaped it.

From vintage field equipment to handcrafted curiosities, DOMO exists to give these objects a second life — catalogued, celebrated, and passed on to those who will cherish them most.

This is not just a shop. It is a department. A place of record. A home for things that deserve to be found.

DEPT. OF MISC. OBJECTS

The Department of Miscellaneous Objects does not sell things. It releases them. Each object in this collection was held for longer than it should have been — by an owner, by a system, by the specific human reluctance to let go of something that once meant something. We acquire what others abandon. We catalogue what others overlook. We keep what the world decided was no longer worth keeping. If you are here, you already understand why that matters. The department did not find you by accident. Nothing in this collection arrived by accident. Every object has a file. Every visitor leaves a trace. Welcome to the record.
